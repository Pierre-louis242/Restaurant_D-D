WHO ARE D&D THEN?

Dolores and Dominique, after many years in the Republic of Congo and an unconditional love of cooking, created the D&D in 2017. It's a tapas and wine restaurant located in the city center of Brazzaville, offering a unique overlook of the Congo River and the capital of DRC, Kinshasa.

OUR FOOD AND WINE

OUR FOOD

Dolores propose to you home made Tapas prepared with fresh and of course the very best ingredients. But let's not forget the most important, they are cooked with love!

OUR WINE

Carefully picked by Dominique, he proposes to you the best selection of wine around France, South-Africa and South-America. Changing enough often so you'll always have the opportunity to discover new products from around the world.
