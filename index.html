WHO ARE D&D?

Dolores and Dominique, after many years in the Republic of Congo and an unconditional love of cooking, created the D&D in 2017. It's a tapas and wine restaurant located in the city center of Brazzaville, offering a unique view over the Congo River and the capital of DRC, Kinshasa.

OUR FOOD AND WINE

OUR FOOD

Dolores proposes home made Tapas prepared with fresh and of course the very best ingredients. But let's not forget the most important, they are made with love!

OUR WINE

Carefully picked by Dominique, he proposes the best selection of wines around France, South-Africa and South-America. Changing often, you'll always have the opportunity to discover new products from all over the world.
